Desserts

Best Ever Banana Bread 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened 1/2 cup sugar 1/4 cup light brown sugar, packed 2 eggs beaten 1 tsp pure vanilla extract 1/2 cup buttermilk (or plain yogurt or sour cream) 3 ripe bananas, mashed 13/4 cup all-purpose flour 1/4 tsp salt 1 tsp baking soda 1/4 tsp cinnamon 1/2 cup chocolate chips or other mix-ins Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugars. Stir in eggs, vanilla, buttermilk, and mashed bananas, blending well. Fold in the flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and chocolate chips. Spoon the miscture into the loaf pain. Bake 55 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool 15 minutes before removing the bread from the pan. Perfect Chocolate chip cookies Ingredients: Wet ingredients 1/2 cup granulated sugar 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed 3/4 cup butter melted 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk 1 Tbs. vanilla extract Dry ingredients 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour 1 tsp. baking soda 1 1/2 tsp. cornstarch 1/2 tsp. salt 1 cup chocolate chips Instructions: Whisk together wet ingredients In a separate bowl sift together dry ingredients Stir together wet and dry ingredients until blended Chill for at least 3 hours When ready to bake, form the dough into balls and bake at 350 degrees for 11-12 minutes Cinnamon rolls Dough 1/2 cup warm water 2 pkgs. active dry yeast 1 Tbs. sugar 1 small pkg. instant vanilla pudding 2 cups milk 1/2 cup butter, melted 2 eggs, beaten 1 tsp. salt 7 cups flour Cinnamon mixture 1/2 cup butter, melted 1 cup sugar 1/4 cup cinnamon Cream cheese frosting 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese frosting 1/2 cup butter, softened 1 tsp. vanilla 3 cups powdered sugar 1 Tbs. milk In a bowl combine water, yeast, and sugar and set aside In a mixer combine pudding with 2 cups milk. Add butter, eggs, and salt, mix well. Add yeast to pudding mixture and mix well. Gradually add flour and knead until smooth and elastic Cover and let rise until doubled in size (about 1 hour) While dough is rising, mix cinnamon and sugar mixture and set aside On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough into 1/4 inch thick rectangle Spread 1/2 cup melted butter over the dough rectangle. Sprinkle the cinnamon mixture over buttered dough and spread evenly. Start rolling the dough from the far side and continue to roll tightly. Slice the dough at 1-2 inch Place dough on greased pan and cover. Allow to rise for another hour Bake @350 degrees for 15-20 minutes Frost with cream cheese frosting.

Knotted Bread Rolls Ingredients: 1/4 cup warm water 1 Tbs. yeast 3/4 cup + 1 tsp. sugar 1 Tbs. salt 1 cup instant potato flakes 8 cups bread flour 3/4 cup butter 3 cups of milk 4 eggs (room temperature) Instructions: 1. In a stand mixer add warm water, yeast, and 1 tsp sugar. Combine and let sit for 10 minutes or until it bubbles. Then add sugar, salt, instant potato flakes, and 2 cups bread flour. Mix together. 2. In a saucepan, melt 3/4 cup butter and add 3 cups of milk. Heat and cool to warm so it wont kill the yeast. Add to yeast mixture. Add 4 eggs and 6 cups of flour. 3. Dough will be sticky. Let rise for 1 hour. Roll out in a rectangle and cover with softened butter. Fold over into a tri-fold, or letter fold, and cut into 1-inch strips. Knot rolls and put onto cookie sheet. Let rise for 3 hours. Bake at 400 degrees for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned and cooked through. Cafe Rio chicken salad Rio Creamy Tomatillo Dressing 1 buttermilk ranch dressing packet (make as per recipe) 2 tomatillos (tomato like vegetable with a husk around them 1/2 bunch of cilantro 1 clove garlic Juice of 1 lime 1 jalapeno (Use the seeds too if you like it spicy. You could substitute a few drops of green tabasco for the jalapeno.) Use a food processor to blend all the ingredients well. Refrigerate. Cafe Rio Chicken 1 small bottle Kraft Zesty Italian Dressing 1 T chili powder 1 T cumin 3 cloves garlic-minced 5 Ibs chicken breast Cook all together in a crock pot for 4 hours, shred meat and cook 1 additional hour. Cafe Rio Rice 4 c water 4 t chicken bouillon 4 t garlic -minced 1/2 bunch cilantro 1 can green chiles-or equivalent fresh 1/2 t salt 1 T butter 1/2 onion 2 c rice Blend cilantro, green chiles and onion together in food processor. Bring water to a boil and add all ingredients, simmer covered 30 minutes. This is typically made in the restaurant with a grilled tortilla with melted cheese and covered with rice, chicken, lettuce, tomato, and dressing. Melt in your mouth biscuits Ingredients 4 cups Pioneer baking mix + extra for rolling 2 cups sour cream 1/4 cup sprite 1/2 stick butter Instructions Heat oven to 375 degrees In a large bowl, mix together biscuit mix and sour cream. Add 7up a little at a time until the dough forms a ball Sprinkle 1/2 cup of baking mix on counter to roll the dough in. Roll dough to 1/2 inch thickness Using a round biscuit cutter, cut biscuits. Brush melted butter in bottom of pan, place biscuits in pan, brush butter on top. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
